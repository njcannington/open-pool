House rules

Best 4 of 6, combined to par. Lowest wins. The other two don't count.
Withdrawal: the alternate takes the spot and brings their own score. One alternate, so only the first withdrawal is covered.
Disqualification: no substitute.
Missed cut: keeps the 36-hole score, then gets charged the worst round in the field for R3 and R4.
